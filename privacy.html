Privacy Policy

This app is a personal analytics tool used solely by its creator. It accesses TikTok video performance data (views, likes, comments, and shares) for personal tracking purposes only.

Data Collection

This app collects TikTok account performance metrics through the TikTok API. No personal data is collected from or shared with any third party.

Data Usage

Data is used exclusively by the app owner to monitor content performance. It is stored in a private Google Sheet and is never sold, shared, or published.
